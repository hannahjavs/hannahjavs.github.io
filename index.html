Back To Top

The Smurf Race is a two player game where the players have to press alternate keys in order to trigger their smurf character to move. There is a 3-2-1 countdown and then “GO!” which flashes when the players can start pressing keys in order to move. During the race along the way the user must press another key in order to jump and squash mushrooms to keep moving. There are sound effects for the squashing of mushrooms and reaching the finish line. A score is tallied and the users can keep track of who is getting to the finish line first.

Mapp App

Project 2

www.mapp-ga.herokuapp.com

Mapp is a social planning app. An application to allow people to create social events and efit proposed events, and to allow creators of events to email out the invitations. This is a MEAN stack application, built in a group of three, with an Angular 1 front end, and making extensive use of the Google Maps, Places and Directions APIs. My role within the team consisted of taking the lead on the Express API back end, and the on route line that was created using markers when clicked by the user. Additionally, I made the design choices such as colour schemes, layout of pages and map style.

Technologies used: Express, js, AngularJS, Google Maps API, Gulp, SCSS, MongoDB with Mongoose models, HTML5
